Videos: 4

Difficulty Level: Intermediate to Advanced

Titan Torque is a power-packed weight training program designed to build raw strength and muscle endurance in the shoulders, back, and arms. This course focuses on progressive overload and compound movements, ensuring users gain both muscular definition and functional strength. Gravity Grip

⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️ (4.4)

Category: Calisthenics

Duration: 12 minutes

Videos: 4

Difficulty Level: Intermediate to Advanced

Gravity Grip is a calisthenics-based forearm and grip strength program designed to build iron-clad grip power and enhance wrist stability. Strong forearms are crucial for pulling strength, lifting heavier weights, and improving overall upper-body endurance. This course engages the forearms, wrists, biceps, and fingers, ensuring a powerful grip for both strength and endurance-based movements.

Exercises:

  • Dead Hangs
  • Towel Grip Pull-Ups
  • Farmer’s Walk
  • Fingertip Push-Ups
  • Wrist Curls

Benefits:

  • Strengthens grip for better lifting and calisthenics performance
  • Improves wrist mobility and reduces injury risk
  • Enhances endurance for sports like rock climbing and combat training

Category: Yoga

Duration: 12 minutes

Videos: 4

Difficulty Level: Beginner to Intermediate

Zen Warrior is a yoga and flexibility-focused course that blends mindfulness, breath control, and controlled movements to enhance mobility, flexibility, and muscle recovery.   • Enhances muscle recovery and reduces soreness
  • Improves mind-body connection and balance
